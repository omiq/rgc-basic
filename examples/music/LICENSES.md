## Other good sources for tracker music

- **The Mod Archive** — `https://modarchive.org/` — filter by
  license = "Public Domain" to find ~1500 safe-to-bundle tunes
  (`https://modarchive.org/index.php?request=view_by_license&query=publicdomain`).
- **Big Mod Music Pack by computermouth** —
  `https://computermouth.itch.io/big-mod-music-pack` — large curated
  bundle for games / demos.
- **archive.org MOD archive** — bulk downloads + Amiga demoscene classics.

Attribution is good practice even for Public Domain work. Keep a
`LICENSES.md` alongside any shipped tracker files so downstream
users know where the tune came from.
